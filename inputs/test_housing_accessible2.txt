--------------------------------------------------
GENERAL OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------
Single-storey accessible-ready home with optional second-storey expansion.
Total Area: 1450 sq ft
Rooms: Living Room, Kitchen, Dining Area, 2 Bedrooms, Accessible Bathroom,
Laundry Room, Meeting/Study Room, Storage, Outdoor Patio.

Designed to exceed accessibility standards in physical access, sensory alerts,
and social inclusivity.

--------------------------------------------------
PHYSICAL ACCESS FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

1. ACCESSIBLE ROUTES
- Exterior pathways: Minimum 1500 mm width.
- Interior pathways: Minimum 1200 mm width.
- No-step entry at main entrance.
- Slip-resistant flooring throughout.

2. DOORS
- Minimum clear doorway width: 900 mm (exceeds 860 mm requirement).
- Maneuvering clearance:
  - Pull side: 600 mm.
  - Push side: 300 mm.
- Level threshold transitions.

3. MANEUVERING SPACE
- Turning radius: Minimum 1800 mm in all main rooms.
- Meeting/Study Room mobility space:
  - 820 mm x 1390 mm stationary space.
  - 1800 mm turning circle.

4. ACCESSIBLE-READY SECOND FLOOR
- Structural framing allows future installation of lift/elevating device.
- Electrical and plumbing pre-routed to allow retrofit without modification.

5. KITCHEN (ACCESSIBLE-READY)
- Adjustable-height counters (future retrofit ready).
- 1200 mm clearance between counters.
- Reinforced walls for grab bars.
- Sink plumbing insulated and positioned for wheelchair access.

6. BATHROOM (ACCESSIBLE-READY)
- Roll-in shower retrofit-ready.
- Bathtub reinforcement for future accessible installation.
- Medicine cabinet adjustable height ready.
- Reinforced walls for grab bars.
- 1800 mm turning circle.

7. BEDROOMS
- Door clearance: 900 mm.
- Bed clearance on both sides: 1200 mm.
- Structural reinforcement for ceiling lift installation.

8. LAUNDRY ROOM
- Front-loading washer/dryer space.
- 1200 mm maneuvering clearance.
- Plumbing/electrical pre-installed for accessible retrofit.

9. OPERATING CONTROLS
- Light switches, thermostats, and controls installed between 400–1100 mm.
- Display panels installed at 1000 mm.
- Future relocation possible between 400–1500 mm.

--------------------------------------------------
SENSORY ALERT FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

- Smoke, fire, and CO detectors include audible + flashing visual alerts.
- Doorbell includes visual strobe signal.
- Smart-home notification system sends alerts to mobile devices.

--------------------------------------------------
SOCIAL HEALTH FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

1. MULTILINGUAL SIGNAGE
- All signage provided in:
  - Plain language English.
  - ASL (American Sign Language) video QR codes.
  - LSQ (Langue des signes québécoise) video QR codes.
- High-contrast, large-font text.
- Braille labels on room signage.

2. COMMUNITY SPACE
- Meeting/Study Room with mobility device access.
- Flexible furniture layout for group interaction.

--------------------------------------------------
ADDITIONAL ACCESSIBILITY ENHANCEMENTS
--------------------------------------------------

- Lever-style door handles.
- Smart voice-controlled lighting.
- Non-glare lighting.

- Accessible outdoor patio with ramp (1:12 slope).

FUTURE-READY DESIGN
--------------------------------------------------
