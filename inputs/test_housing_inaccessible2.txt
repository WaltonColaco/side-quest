-------------------------------------------------
GENERAL OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------
Single-storey bungalow with unfinished basement.
Total Area: 1350 sq ft
Rooms: Living Room, Kitchen, Dining Area, 2 Bedrooms,
1 Bathroom, Laundry Closet, Small Office.

Some accessibility ideas included, but not designed to full standards.

--------------------------------------------------
PHYSICAL ACCESS FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

1. ACCESSIBLE ROUTES
- Rear entrance has small ramp (1:10 slope).
- Front entrance has 2 steps.
- Interior hallways: 1000 mm width (below recommended 1200 mm).
- Exterior path width: 1100 mm.

2. DOORS
- Main doors: 820 mm width.
- Bedroom doors: 800 mm width.
- Limited maneuvering clearance at bathroom door.

3. MANEUVERING SPACE
- Living room allows ~1500 mm turning space.
- Kitchen aisle: 1000 mm.
- Other rooms tight.

4. SECOND FLOOR / BASEMENT ACCESS
- Basement stairs only.
- No lift retrofit planning.

5. KITCHEN
- Lowered section of counter at 800 mm height.
- Sink cabinet removable for wheelchair access.
- No full accessible-ready layout.

6. BATHROOM
- Grab bar backing installed near toilet.
- Walk-in shower planned but narrow (900 mm).
- Turning radius limited.

7. BEDROOMS
- Wider space on one side of bed.
- Closet doors standard width.
- No ceiling lift reinforcement.

8. LAUNDRY
- Front-loading washer/dryer.
- Tight clearance around machines.

9. OPERATING CONTROLS
- Some switches at 1100 mm.
- Others at 1300 mm.
- Thermostat at 1200 mm.

--------------------------------------------------
SENSORY ALERT FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

- Smoke detectors with audible alarm.
- One visual strobe in hallway only.
- No visual doorbell or CO alarm strobe.

--------------------------------------------------
SOCIAL HEALTH FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

- Some signage in plain-language English.
- Large-font labels in kitchen.
- No ASL or LSQ materials.
- No braille signage.

--------------------------------------------------
ADDITIONAL FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

- Lever handles on main doors only.
- Slip-resistant bathroom floor.
- One parking space with extra side clearance.
