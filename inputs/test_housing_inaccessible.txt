--------------------------------------------------
GENERAL OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------
Two-storey house.
Total Area: 1400 sq ft
Rooms: Living Room, Kitchen, Dining Room, 3 Bedrooms,
1 Bathroom upstairs, Laundry in basement.

Designed as a typical home with minimal accessibility considerations.

--------------------------------------------------
PHYSICAL ACCESS FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

1. ACCESSIBLE ROUTES
- Front entrance has 3 steps and no ramp.
- Exterior walkway width: 900 mm.
- Interior hallways: 800 mm.

2. DOORS
- Doorway widths: 760 mm.
- No extra maneuvering space.

3. MANEUVERING SPACE
- Rooms sized for standard furniture only.
- No turning radius planned.

4. SECOND FLOOR ACCESS
- Only access is stairs.
- No structural allowance for lift installation.

5. KITCHEN
- Fixed-height counters.
- Narrow aisle (900 mm).
- No accessible-ready planning.

6. BATHROOM
- Standard bathtub/shower combo.
- No reinforcement for grab bars.
- Small layout with tight clearance.

7. BEDROOMS
- Standard layout.
- No lift reinforcement.
- Limited clearance around beds.

8. LAUNDRY ROOM
- Located in basement with stairs only.
- Tight space.

9. OPERATING CONTROLS
- Light switches at 1400 mm height.
- Thermostat at 1500 mm.
- No relocation planning.

--------------------------------------------------
SENSORY ALERT FEATURES

- Standard smoke alarms with sound only.
- No visual alerts.
- Standard doorbell only.

--------------------------------------------------
SOCIAL HEALTH FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------

- No multilingual signage.
- No braille labels.
- No ASL or LSQ materials.

--------------------------------------------------
ADDITIONAL FEATURES
